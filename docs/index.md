# MileLog

**Simple, private mileage tracking for tax deductions.**

MileLog helps you track business miles for tax purposes with automatic trip detection. All your data stays on your device - no accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking.

## Features

- **Automatic Trip Detection** - Uses your phone's motion sensors to detect when you're driving and automatically records trips
- **Manual Entry** - Add trips manually with odometer readings or direct distance input
- **Trip Classification** - Categorize trips as Business or Personal with a simple swipe
- **Tax Deduction Tracking** - Automatically calculates your deduction using the current IRS mileage rate
- **Export Reports** - Generate reports for tax filing

## Privacy First

MileLog is designed with privacy as a core principle:

- All data stored locally on your device
- No user accounts required
- No cloud sync or backup
- No analytics or tracking
- No data sharing with third parties
